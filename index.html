Rocket League - Predicting Playstyle & Rank

Using data sourced from the community run website: ballchasing.com, produced an application able to predict which example players (from the three above) a 3v3 rocket league replay resembles. Designed an algorithm to produce playstyle plots from raw data, and used classification modeling techniques to predict playstyles, including:

Natural Language Processing - Liverpool & QPR Subreddits

Utilised the pushshift API to scrape posts and comments from two football communities. Used a variety of
classification techniques to accurately predict which group a post or comment originated from. Analysed dialect
differences and compared community activity over a ten year period, particularly looking at the effect
of the teams external success on activity and positivity. Achieved a balanced accuracy score of 92%,
using a Stacking Classifcation model combining Multinomial Naive Bayes and Logistic Regression.

Electric Vehicles - Air Quality Analysis

Performed in depth analysis on annual electric vehicle registrations and charging station installments, comparing
these figures to yearly air quality data. Time-series modeling was performed to predict future pollution levels, and
specific states were selected for further examination. Regression modeling was used to predict the required number of
charging station installments required to meet global pollution standards set by the UN.
